# Fruit shakes

## Ingredients

|             | Ingredient |
|---          | --- |
|200g         | fruits |
|1/2l         | Milk |
|1 small cup  | yoghurt |
|2 tbsp       | lemon juice |
|3 tbsp       | powdered sugar |
|             |ice cubes depending on temperature |

## Directions

Put all Ingredients except the ice into the blender.
When blending is finished, add Ice until consistency and temperature are where you want them.
